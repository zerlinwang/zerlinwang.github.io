Hello! I am a Master student (since 2021) in Shenzhen International Graduate School of Tsinghua University, advised by [Prof. Zhiyong Wu](https://www.sigs.tsinghua.edu.cn/zywu/main.htm). I received my Bachelor's Degree at Dalian University of Technology. Click [here](https://zerlinwang.github.io/files/zilinwang_resume.pdf) to view my up-to-data CV.

My research goal is to construct general decision-making system capable of interacting with complex environments.
Currently, I focus on Reinforcement Learning (RL) as a solution method.
I have delved into areas such as multi-agent coordination, visual RL, learning from sub-optimal demonstrations, RL in content generation and autonomous driving.
Apart from that, I also have some publications in object detection and speech processing.
