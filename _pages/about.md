Hello! I am a Master student (since 2021) in Shenzhen International Graduate School of Tsinghua University, advised by [Prof. Zhiyong Wu](https://www.sigs.tsinghua.edu.cn/zywu/main.htm). I received my Bachelor's Degree at Dalian University of Technology. Click [here](https://zerlinwang.github.io/files/zilinwang_resume.pdf) to view my up-to-data CV.

Nowadays, my research interest has revolved around reinforcement learning, especially its usage in multi-agent system (MARL) and offline scenarios (offline RL).
In these years, with the strong expressive power of deep neural networks, reinforcement learning has achieved some amazing successes including game playing, robot controlling and self-driving, etc.
I believe that reinforcement learning is a key step to artificial general intelligence (AGI)!
The goal of my research is to achieve the generalizability of the reinforcement learning algorithms, designing reinforcement learning algorithms that are decoupled from the specific tasks and applicable to any environments.
